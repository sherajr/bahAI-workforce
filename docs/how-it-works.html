bahAI Workforce — How It Works

You give the team a theme. Four AI agents consult together, create the artwork and listing, hold each other to the constitution, and hand you a finished bookmark.

The Team

One Bookmark, Start to Finish

1

You give a theme You

e.g. "The unity of humanity" — typed into the dashboard's Pipeline tab.

▼
2

Citations retrieved Librarian

Searches a local index of the actual writings for passages that fit the theme.

▼
3

Artwork created Artist

Builds an image prompt from the theme + citations and generates the painting.

▼
4

Consultation — two rounds All four

Artist, Scribe, Reviewer, and Librarian each speak in turn, guided by cited excerpts from Consultation: A Compilation. Round 1 surfaces the clash of opinions; round 2 turns conjecture into certitude and commits to a quote and direction. The decision becomes binding on everyone.

▼
5

Listing written Scribe

Writes the full Etsy listing around the team's chosen quote (which is locked — nobody may change it).

▼
↻ improve until the score is high enough
6

Scored against the constitution Reviewer

Looks at the real artwork and the listing, scores all 9 principles, and writes exact find-and-replace fixes.

▼
7

Fixes applied Scribe + code

The Reviewer's edits are applied mechanically by code (so nothing gets ignored), then an honesty filter strips any claim that can't be verified.

▼
8

Product finished System

Saved to the database, rendered as print-ready 2×6″ front (quote overlay) and back PNGs, and sent to your Canva template. From the Products tab you can download the files, ask the team to improve it, edit it by hand, or publish a draft to Etsy.

Where the Money Goes

Free — runs on your PC

  • Scribe's writing (local Qwen model)
  • Librarian's search & verification
  • Database, dashboard, bookmark rendering

Paid — xAI API

  • Image generation (one per bookmark)
  • Reviewer scoring (needs to see the art)
  • Artist's visual check of the artwork
